20 Movies Every Entrepreneur Must Watch for Inspiration | Best Business Movies

  • by Anurag Jain
  • February 7, 2023
  • 0 Comments
  • 9 minutes read
  • 679 Views
  • 11 months ago
Facebook Twitter Pinterest Whatsapp StumbleUpon Print Share via Email

Ready for a movie night that’ll take your entrepreneurial skills to the next level? Put down that business book and grab some popcorn, because we’re about to explore the exciting world of entrepreneurship through the lens of Movies.

From inspiring tales of success to cautionary tales of failure, movies have a lot to offer entrepreneurs of all levels.

Movies have the unique ability to inspire, motivate and educate us in a fun and engaging way. For entrepreneurs, watching movies can provide valuable lessons and insights on business strategy, leadership, and personal growth. They can offer a fresh perspective, challenge our assumptions, and inspire new ideas. Whether you’re a seasoned entrepreneur or just starting out, there’s always something to be learned from watching movies.

 

Best Movies for Entrepreneurs to Elevate the Leadership Quotient

Watching movies can have a profound impact on entrepreneurs and their businesses. Here are some ways that entrepreneurs have benefited from watching movies in real life:

1. Gaining inspiration – Many entrepreneurs have found inspiration from movies, such as “The Social Network” and “The Pursuit of Happyness,” that showcase the challenges and triumphs of entrepreneurs. These movies can provide entrepreneurs with the motivation they need to pursue their own goals and dreams.

2. Building leadership skills – Movies such as “The Wolf of Wall Street” and “The Big Short” can provide valuable lessons on leadership, decision-making, and risk-taking. Entrepreneurs can use these lessons to develop their own leadership skills and become more effective in their roles.

3. Learning about teamwork and collaboration – Movies such as “The Startup” highlight the importance of teamwork and collaboration in achieving success. Entrepreneurs can learn from these movies about the importance of working together and building strong relationships with their colleagues and partners.

4. Developing a growth mindset – Many successful entrepreneurs have a growth mindset, meaning they view challenges and failures as opportunities to learn and grow. Movies such as “The Pursuit of Happyness” can inspire entrepreneurs to adopt a growth mindset and become more resilient in the face of adversity.

5. Improving communication skills – Effective communication is critical for entrepreneurs, and movies such as “The Social Network” and “The Big Short” can provide valuable lessons on the art of persuasion and influence. Entrepreneurs can use these lessons to improve their own communication skills and become more effective in negotiations and sales.

Movies can provide valuable lessons and insights for entrepreneurs, inspiring us to think creatively and find success in their ventures. So sit back, relax, and get ready to discover why watching movies is a must-do for any entrepreneur looking to grow their business and reach new heights.

 

List of Must Watch Movies for Entrepreneurs and Business Owners

Here are some of the best movies every entrepreneur should watch, along with memorable quotes from each one:

 

1. The Social Network (2010)

This movie tells the story of Mark Zuckerberg and the creation of Facebook. It provides a unique insight into the early days of a startup and the challenges faced by entrepreneurs in the tech industry.

Quote: “The internet’s not written in pencil, Mark, it’s written in ink.”

2. The Pursuit of Happyness (2006)

This movie is based on the true story of Chris Gardner, a struggling salesman who eventually became a successful entrepreneur. The film showcases the determination and resilience required to succeed as an entrepreneur.

Quote: “Don’t ever let someone tell you that you can’t do something. Not even me.”

 

3. The Wolf of Wall Street (2013)

This movie is based on the life of Jordan Belfort, a stockbroker who rose to fame and fortune before eventually serving time in prison for securities fraud. The film provides a cautionary tale about the dangers of greed and corruption in business.

Inspiring Quote from the movie: “The only thing standing between you and your dream is the story you keep telling yourself that you can’t achieve it.”

 

4. Moneyball (2011)

This movie is based on the true story of Billy Beane, the general manager of the Oakland Athletics baseball team. Beane used innovative thinking and data analysis to build a winning team on a budget, and the film showcases the importance of innovation and outside-the-box thinking in business.

Quote: “When the people who are responsible for a system are making a killing from that system, it’s hard to get them to change it.”

 

5. The Founder (2016)

This film tells the story of Ray Kroc, who transformed McDonald’s from a small burger chain into a global franchise empire. It provides insights into the challenges and opportunities of building a successful franchise business.

Quote: “Ideas are easy, execution is everything.”

 

6. The Big Short (2015)

This movie provides an in-depth look at the 2008 financial crisis, and the efforts of several individuals who predicted and profited from the collapse of the housing market. The film showcases the importance of innovative thinking and the dangers of ignoring warning signs in business.

Quote: “In the end, the people who really make money are the ones who show up.”

 

7. The Incredibles (2004)

Now, this is a fun movie you can watch with your family. In this movie, a family of superheroes learns the importance of teamwork, determination, and the power of positive thinking in overcoming adversity.

Quote: “It’s not about winning or losing. It’s about working together.”

 

8. Boiler Room (2000)

This movie tells the story of a young man who joins a brokerage firm and is quickly drawn into a world of greed and corruption. The film provides a cautionary tale about the importance of ethics in business and the dangers of cutting corners.

Interesting Quote from this movie: Jim Young : [to the new recruits] “And there is no such thing as a no sale call. A sale is made on every call you make. Either you sell the client some stock or he sells you a reason he can’t. Either way a sale is made, the only question is who is gonna close?”

 

9. Jerry Maguire (1996)

This film follows the journey of Jerry Maguire, a sports agent who starts his own firm after being fired from his previous job. The movie showcases the importance of passion and perseverance in pursuing your dreams and building a successful business.

Quote: “ The key to this business is personal relationships.”

 

10. Steve Jobs (2015)

This film is a biographical drama about the life and legacy of Steve Jobs, the co-founder of Apple. The movie provides insight into Jobs’ creative and innovative approach to business and the impact of his vision on the tech industry.

Quote: “Design is not just what it looks like and feels like. Design is how it works.”

 

11. The Martian (2015)

This film follows the journey of an astronaut who is stranded on Mars and must use his ingenuity and resourcefulness to survive. The movie showcases the importance of problem-solving skills, resilience, and determination in business.

Quote: “Every time something goes wrong, the world forgets why we fly.”

 

12. Dead Poet's Society (1989)

This film tells the story of an unorthodox English teacher who inspires his students to think creatively and pursue their passions. The movie provides inspiration for entrepreneurs to think outside the box and pursue their passions with passion and determination.

Quote: “Carpe diem. Seize the day, boys. Make your lives extraordinary.”

 

13. Good Will Hunting (1997)

This film is about a janitor with a genius-level intellect who is discovered by a psychologist and begins to pursue his potential. The movie showcases the importance of recognizing and pursuing your talents, as well as the role of mentorship in personal and professional growth.

Quote: “You’ll have bad times, but it’ll always wake you up to the good stuff you weren’t paying attention to.”

 

14. The Secret Life of Walter Mitty (2013)

This film tells the story of a timid and unadventurous man who embarks on a journey of self-discovery. The movie showcases the importance of taking risks and pursuing your dreams, even if they seem impossible.

Quote: “To see the world, things dangerous to come to, to see behind walls, draw closer, to find each other, and to feel. That is the purpose of life.”

 

15. The Imitation Game (2014)

This film is based on the life of Alan Turing, a mathematician who cracked the German Enigma code during World War II. The movie highlights the importance of perseverance, innovation, and problem-solving skills in business.

Quote: “Sometimes it is the people no one imagines anything of who do the things that no one can imagine.”

 

16. The Intern (2015)

In this movie, a 70-year-old intern at an online fashion site learns valuable lessons about the importance of hard work, mentorship, and staying relevant in the ever-changing business world.

Quote: “If you don’t have any shadows, you’re not in the sunshine.”

 

17. Rocky (1976)

In this movie, a small-time boxer gets a shot at the world heavyweight championship and learns the importance of perseverance, determination, and the power of positive thinking.

Quote: “It’s not how hard you hit, it’s about how hard you can get hit and keep moving forward.”

 

18. Up in the Air (2009)

In this movie, a corporate downsizer learns the importance of finding meaning and purpose in life, and the dangers of living a life without personal connections.

Quote: “If you want to be a millionaire, you need to start thinking like one.”

 

19. The Prestige (2006)

In this movie, two rival magicians engage in a battle of wits, and highlight the importance of creativity, determination, and the ability to adapt in a competitive environment.

Quote: “Every great magic trick consists of three parts or acts.”

 

20. The Aviator (2004)

This movie tells the story of Howard Hughes and his legendary career as a filmmaker and aviator, and highlights the importance of creativity, determination, and the power of positive thinking.

Quote: “If you can do something better than anyone else, it means you have a talent.”

 

These movies are not only entertaining, but also educational, providing valuable lessons and inspiration for entrepreneurs. By watching these films, entrepreneurs can gain a better understanding of the challenges and rewards of starting a business and the qualities required to succeed.

 

Conclusion – Best Business Movies for Entrepreneurs

In conclusion, watching movies is a fun and engaging way for entrepreneurs to learn and grow. From inspiring tales of success to cautionary tales of failure, movies have a wealth of lessons to offer. They can provide valuable insights, challenge our assumptions, inspire new ideas, and help us develop critical skills.

So next time you’re looking to boost your motivation, gain new perspectives, or improve your leadership abilities, consider settling in for a movie night. With so many great films to choose from, you’re sure to find one that will inspire and motivate you on your entrepreneurial journey

So, grab some popcorn, settle in, and get ready to learn and grow as an entrepreneur!
